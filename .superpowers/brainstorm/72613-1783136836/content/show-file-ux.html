The one open question: what happens at the moment the file is shown?

Your note "the card is like normal click file and view" can be read two ways — pick the behavior for the live moment (the card itself is identical in both):

A

Auto-open on arrival (phone ①)

When the agent shows a file, the reader sheet opens by itself — you're already reading when the phone buzzes. Happens once per show; scrollback/reload never re-opens; swipe down and the card (phone ②) remains for later.

B

Never auto-open — card only (phone ②)

The agent's show_file drops the card in the transcript; nothing pops up. You tap the card when you're ready to read. Zero interruption, one extra tap every time.
